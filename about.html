Our story

Frenqualy Asked Questions

How do travel agents get paid, and will it cost me extra?

Great question! Travel agents are typically paid by the resorts, so we don’t charge a service fee for our expertise. Many resorts follow price parity, meaning the cost is the same whether you book through us, directly with the resort, or via an online booking site. The difference? When you book with us, you gain a trusted advisor to help you plan a stress-free, personalized vacation without the hassle of navigating all the details on your own. It’s all about making your travel experience seamless and unforgettable!

Why should I book with you instead of an online booking site or directly with a resort?

Booking with us means having a dedicated travel expert by your side every step of the way—before, during, and after your trip. Here’s why we stand out: Personalized Service: We help plan your dream vacation while keeping your budget in mind. No pressure, no stress—just the perfect getaway you can truly enjoy. Honest Advice: We provide unbiased recommendations about resorts and destinations to ensure you choose the one that’s right for you. Exclusive Perks: Thanks to our strong relationships with resorts, we can often secure perks like room upgrades or special touches that online booking sites can’t offer. Peace of Mind: With your personal 1-800 number to reach us anytime during your trip, help is just a call away if you need it. When you book with us, you get so much more than a reservation—you get a trusted partner in crafting a stress-free, unforgettable vacation.

Will I pay more for my vacation if I book through you instead of an online booking site

Not at all! We have access to the same pricing as online booking sites and can price-match when comparing identical options. In many cases, we can even secure exclusive discounts or promotional offers that aren’t available to the general public. Booking with us means you get competitive pricing plus the added value of personalized service, expert advice, and potential perks—all at no extra cost to you!

Do I have to pay you upfront in full for my vacation?

No, you don’t! The deposit requirements are determined by the resorts or tour operators we work with and vary based on your chosen package and travel dates.
